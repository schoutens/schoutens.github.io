RESEARCH INTEREST

  • Algebraic Model Theory: Definability and constructibility in algebraic geometry and commutative algebra, transfer principles, uniform bounds, Artin Approximation, dimension theory, generalized Grothendieck groups.
  • Commutative Algebra: Tight closure, big Cohen-Macaulay algebras, homological conjectures, number of generators of an ideal, flatness, local rings of finite embedding dimension, order dimension, Gorenstein hulls.
  • Algebraic Geometry: Constructible properties and invariants, set-theoretic complete intersections, rational singularities, vanishing theorems, classification of singularities, motivic rationality, infinite cycles.
  • Rigid Analytic Geometry: Rigid subanalytic sets, flattening.
  • Valuation theory: singular valuations, average of a valuation and value semi-groups, total blow-up ring.
